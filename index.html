About

"Mine" fast food chain was founded on September 1, 2023. Its first branch was opened in Ajou University. "Mine" mainly serves customers through fast food and drinks. Mainly its menu consists of hot dog, donar and coffee. You can also enjoy other products. "Mine" team works only to share happiness with you. Be happy with “Mine”.
